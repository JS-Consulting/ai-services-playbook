Our Engagement

How a partnership unfolds.

Step 01
Coffee-first discovery

Start with a Conversation

We meet to understand how your firm is using AI today, then benchmark it against what we see across mid-market investment firms — drawn from survey data and experts embedded inside your peers. You leave the first meeting knowing where you stand and where the industry is heading.

Step 02
Targeted first workshop

Prove Value Before We Scale

We identify low-hanging fruit and run a hands-on workshop built around your team's actual workflows. The first use cases have to land — they define whether AI feels like a tool or a tax. We don't push for a broader engagement until we've shown real value on a problem that matters to you.

Step 03
Sustainable adoption, not one-off wins

Build for Longevity

AI fatigue is real, and novelty alone doesn't drive returns. We install the systems, rituals, and reinforcement loops that keep adoption alive — nurturing capability across your team over months and years, not weeks.

Step 04
A network of top-tier firms

Join the Peer Circle

As your practice matures, we connect you into a trusted circle of mid-market firms working at the frontier of AI — a place to exchange what's working, what's not, and what's next.
