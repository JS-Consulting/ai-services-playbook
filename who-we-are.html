05Why us

The commercial model is the message.

Legacy consulting bills by the hour. The longer the engagement, the better the margin. We bill one flat fee per sprint, and the work closes when the artifact ships.

Them
McKinsey & Company BCG Deloitte PwC KPMG Tenex.co

Day rates. Time and materials. Multi-year retainers. Junior teams billed at partner-adjacent rates. The economics reward duration, not delivery.

Us

One flat fee per sprint. Development, training, and reporting included. No change orders, no team expansions, no retainer creep. When the artifact is shipped, the engagement is closed.

How we operate

Three commitments.

01

Both founders, always

Every workshop, every sprint, every executive briefing has both of us present. No associate-led delivery. No handoff after the sales call.

02

Artifacts over decks

Every engagement ships working tooling, a trained team, or a specific roadmap. If we cannot name the artifact, the engagement does not start.

03

Bounded capacity

We run a small number of engagements concurrently – by design. If we are full when you call, we will tell you and point you elsewhere.
