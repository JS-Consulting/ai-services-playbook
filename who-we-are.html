How we operate

Three commitments.

01

Both founders, always

Every workshop, every sprint, every executive briefing has both of us present. No associate-led delivery. No handoff after the sales call.

02

Artifacts over decks

Every engagement ships working tooling, a trained team, or a specific roadmap. If we cannot name the artifact, the engagement does not start.

03

Bounded capacity

We run a small number of engagements concurrently – by design. If we are full when you call, we will tell you and point you elsewhere.
