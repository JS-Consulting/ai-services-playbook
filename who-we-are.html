01Project Team Model

AI Native team, pulling from
functional & workflow expertise.

Every engagement pairs workflow-design engineers who shape and ship the AI system with functional experts who have actually run the process being redesigned, drawn from our global network. The pairing is the model – neither a pure consulting team nor a pure engineering team, and never a hand-off between the two.

Function Domain Experts

Senior practitioners who have run finance, procurement, clinical operations, or deal teams. They translate AI capability into workflow change that survives audit and board.

AI Process Engineers

Forward-deployed engineers responsible for the agentic systems, retrieval pipelines, evals, and integration plumbing that turn the design into a production artifact.

Adoption Managers

Engagement leads who own the change, training, and post-launch metrics. They keep the workflow in daily use ninety days after the sprint closes.
02Why us

AI native commercials, tied to delivering outcomes not ideas.

Legacy consulting bills by the hour. The longer the engagement, the better the margin. We bill one flat fee per sprint, and the work closes when the artifact ships.

Them

Time and materials billing. Charge for strategy, planning, and development separately. The economics reward duration, not delivery.

Us

One flat fee per sprint. Development, training, and reporting included. No change orders, no team expansions, no retainer creep. When the artifact is shipped, the engagement is closed.
