AI Adoption Programme

6-week sprints · Minimum 2 sprints · The flagship

Revolving sprint-based AI adoption running top-down and bottom-up simultaneously. Each sprint focuses on developing, piloting, and training two AI use cases. Two senior consultants in every session. No artificial end point.

4–6 firms · Peer-group format

A lower-cost group-delivery model. We work with each firm's designated AI champion across a cohort of non-competing firms. Shared workshops, firm-specific office hours, and a shared resource library. Available from Q4 2026.
