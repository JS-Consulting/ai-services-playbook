03Our Engagement

How we partner with you.

We don't scale an engagement until a real workflow is landing. Every step has to earn the next.

Step 01 / Discovery
Coffee-first conversation

Start with a conversation

We meet to understand how your firm uses AI today and benchmark against what we see across mid-market investment firms — survey data plus experts embedded inside your peers.

Step 02 / Pilot
Targeted first workshop

Prove value before we scale

We identify low-hanging fruit and run a hands-on workshop on your team's real workflows. The first use case has to land — that defines whether AI feels like a tool or a tax.

Step 03 / Install
Sustainable adoption

Build for longevity

We install the systems, rituals and reinforcement loops that keep adoption alive — nurturing capability over months and years, not weeks. AI fatigue is real; novelty alone doesn't drive returns.

Step 04 / Network
A network of top-tier firms

Join the peer circle

As your practice matures, we connect you into a trusted circle of mid-market firms working at the frontier — a place to trade what's working, what's not and what's next.
